ETINMIP data 

In GitHub repository, long term mean atmospheric data is uploaded
CTL - 31-150 year mean
NEXT - 101-150 year mean
SEXT - 101-150 year mean
STRO - 101-150 year mean

For MIROC model, STRO experiment does not exist
For NORESM model, NEXT experiment does not exist
